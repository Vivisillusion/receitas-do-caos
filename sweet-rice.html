6. Sweet Rice

This is one of those recipes that doesn’t need measuring cups — it asks only for instinct, warmth, and a little cinnamon. A soul recipe. Made to soothe, not impress.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Condensed milk
  • Table cream
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a pot, cook the rice in water — without sugar. Trust me, sugar too early makes the rice stubborn and undercooked. (I've learned the hard way, it doesn't cook!)
  2. Once the rice is soft and fully cooked, lower the heat and pour in the milk.
  3. Add sugar to taste, a generous scoop of condensed milk, a swirl of table cream, and a pinch of cinnamon.
  4. Let everything simmer gently, stirring now and then, until it thickens into creamy, sweet comfort.
  5. Serve warm or cold, with more cinnamon sprinkled over the top like a final kiss.
