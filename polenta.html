Polenta and Minced Meat Sauce

Here's a very simple Polenta and Minced Meat Sauce recipe that has saved my life so many times:

Ingredients

  • water
  • corn meal
  • minced meat
  • seasoning
  • oil
  • tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine your water and corn meal
  2. Set the heat to low
  3. Stir constantly until it boils and thickens

For the Meat Sauce

  1. Cook the minced meat in a pan
  2. Add seasoning and oil, fry until browned
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce and let it simmer
