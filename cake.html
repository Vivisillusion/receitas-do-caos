8. Gluten Free corn meal Cake

This humble cake has fed generations in Brazil. It’s rustic, golden, and smells like home. Naturally gluten-free, it rises into a crumbly, tender dream, and the cornmeal crust around the edges? Utter perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 American glass (approx. 200ml) of oil
  • 1 American glass of milk
  • 2 American glasses of sugar
  • 2 American glasses of cornmeal
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a blender, mix the eggs, oil, and milk until smooth.
  3. Slowly add the sugar, cornmeal, and salt, blending well after each addition.
  4. Finally, stir in the baking powder gently—don’t overmix.
  5. Grease a cake pan with oil and sprinkle it with cornmeal instead of flour for a crispy, golden crust.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for about 40 minutes, or until the top is golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
