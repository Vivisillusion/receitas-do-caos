9. Chicken Fricasse

This is the kind of recipe that feels like a cozy hug with claws. Velvety, cheesy, golden on top and the oven does most of the work while you lounge in your chaos. Serve it hot, and don’t expect leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breast, cut into cubes (stroganoff-style)
  • 1 jar of requeijão (or cream cheese spread)
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 box of table cream (creme de leite)
  • 1 packet of powdered onion soup mix
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Spread the cubed chicken in a baking dish and season with a bit of salt. Set aside.
  2. In a blender, mix the requeijão, corn, table cream, and onion soup powder until smooth.
  3. Pour the creamy mixture over the chicken, making sure it’s well covered.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 40 minutes or until golden on top.
  5. Right before serving, cover it generously with crispy shoestring potatoes. That crunch? It’s divine.
  6. Put it over rice, mashed potatoes, or just eat it with a spoon straight from the dish. No shame.
