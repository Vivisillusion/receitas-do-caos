2. Spinach and Minced Meat Soup

This is one of my comfort soups—soft, warm, and easy to digest. I call it a baby soup, not just because it’s blended, but because it’s gentle. It takes care of you.

Ingredients

  • Water (enough to cover the ingredients)
  • A handful of fresh spinach leaves
  • Minced meat (any you prefer—beef, chicken, turkey)
  • Seasonings to taste (salt, herbs, or a mild broth cube)

Instructions

  1. Place the water, spinach, minced meat, and seasoning into a pot.
  2. Simmer over medium heat until the meat is fully cooked and tender.
  3. Remove from heat. Carefully transfer everything into a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Serve warm. Add a drizzle of olive oil or a sprinkle of herbs if you’d like.
